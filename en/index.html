HOW LONG DOES THE FREIGHT BECOME ARRIVING?

Upon receipt of your load at our facilities in Miami Venecargo Xpress will prepare your transportation, customs clearance and delivery. The day of air load is on Tuesdays and Fridays the arrival time to destination is on average 3 to 4 business days and for maritime load are on Fridays the arrival time to destination is on average from 3 to 4 weeks, this can vary by customs procedures.

ARE THERE SENDING RESTRICTIONS?

If there are products of prohibited and / or restricted such as perishable food without sanitary registration, printed material that can be considered subversive, alcohols and tobacco, weapons (both fire and white), chemical products and explosive liquids, cocaine, morphine, marijuana, opium and other narcotics, live or dead animals and cash.
